Welcome to Danfe Badminton Society

Danfe badminton society was formed in September 2018. Our aim is to reach out to the local community and bring them out on the field and get them involve in some physical activities apart from their day to day life. Badminton offers a whole host of benefits ranging from increasing life expectancy and mobility to promoting heart health and exercise for all ages and abilities.
